## Critical Issues

### 1. **17 Sections is Too Many**

**Typical high-converting landing page:** 5-7 sections
**This page:** 17 sections

**User attention span:** ~30 seconds before fatigue sets in.
**By section 8:** User has already seen: Hook → Problem → $199 → Mentoring → Stats → Research → About
**Result:** They're overwhelmed before they see social proof.

### 2. **Redundant Social Proof (6 Sections!)**

| Section | What It Proves | Redundant With |
|---------|----------------|----------------|
| KeyStatsSection | "40+ AI systems, 6 certifications" | OriginalResearch |
| OriginalResearchSection | "72% cite workflow, 67% fail" | KeyStats |
| FeaturedInSection | Logo credibility | Testimonials |
| WhyWorkWithMeSection | Methodology | AboutMe |
| TestimonialsSection | Client results | KeyStats |
| Case Studies teaser | Implementation results | Testimonials |

**Recommendation:** Consolidate into 2 sections:
- **Trust Section:** KeyStats + FeaturedIn logos
- **Proof Section:** Testimonials + Case Studies link

### 3. **ChallengeSection Doesn't Add Value**

**What it does:** Explains "Why AI adoption fails"
**What Hero already says:** "Intelligence is cheap. Trust is the new scarcity. I help you stop chasing AI tools and start building systems that actually work."

**Analysis:**
- The hero already establishes the problem ("trust is scarce") and the promise ("building systems that work")
- ChallengeSection adds THREE more paragraphs explaining what the Hero already implied
- The "three signals" (Human Architect, High-Vocabulary Prompting, Last Mile Polishing) are abstract concepts that don't convert
- No CTA in ChallengeSection - user just scrolls past

**Verdict:** ❌ **REMOVE or collapse into Hero**

### 4. **Multiple Competing Offers**

| Section | Offer | CTA | Problem |
|---------|-------|-----|---------|
| Hero | Strategy call | "Book Free Strategy Call" | Primary CTA |
| Spotlight | $199 landing page | "Claim Your $199 Page" | Competes with Hero |
| Mentoring | $97-$497 sessions | "Book a Mentoring Session" | Competes with Spotlight |
| Assessment | Free quiz | "Take Free Assessment" | Competes with Hero |
| Services | Full catalog | Expandable accordion | Buried below fold |
| Connect | Contact | "Book Free Strategy Call" | Redundant with Hero |

**Result:** User doesn't know what action to take.

**Recommendation:**
- Primary CTA: "Book Free Strategy Call" (Hero, Connect)
- Secondary CTA: "Take Assessment" (lower in page)
- Remove Spotlight and Mentoring from homepage OR move to Services

### 5. **SpeakingSection Wrong Audience**

- Speaking engagements are a **secondary revenue stream**
- Most visitors need AI consulting, not a speaker
- SpeakingSection dilutes the core offer
- Takes up screen real estate for non-converting audience

**Recommendation:** ❌ **REMOVE from homepage** - Link from About page instead
