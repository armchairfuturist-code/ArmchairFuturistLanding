# **App Name**: Armchair Futurist

## Core Features:

- Hero Section: A compelling hero section that grabs attention and clearly articulates the value proposition of Alex Myers as an advisor, speaker, and consultant. Features a professional photo and clear calls-to-action (CTAs) to 'Schedule a Discovery Call' and 'Invite Me to Speak'.
- Trust Indicators: Showcase logos of organizations Alex has partnered with to build trust and credibility. Prominently display credentials and certifications like CCMP, FLTA, CEBP, PSM, and PAL to highlight expertise.
- Advisory Services: Advisory services: detail the offer and clarify who it is best suited for. Describe Advisory & Mentoring, AI Change Programs, Speaking & Roundtables, Education & Training, and Facilitation offers. Optimize descriptions with respect to AI.
- AI Email Introduction: AI-powered tool that uses machine learning to generate personalized introductory emails to prospective clients, leveraging data from their LinkedIn profiles, recent posts, and company news to tailor the message, increasing engagement.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, trustworthy blue (#34495E) to convey professionalism and intelligence. The color should have good contrast against both light and dark text.
- Background color: A light gray (#ECF0F1) to ensure readability and a clean, modern look. It echoes the primary blue in hue, but is extremely desaturated and bright.
- Accent color: A warm, approachable orange (#E67E22) used for CTAs and highlights to draw attention without feeling overly aggressive. It provides good visual contrast and ties in well with a blue color scheme, as it is an analogous color.
- A clean, professional font to balance approachability with readability. One font choice should be selected, to establish consistency of style.
- A grid-based layout to present content in an organized manner.
- Subtle, professional animations to subtly reveal content and make the UI more attractive. Animations should not distract or disrupt readability.
